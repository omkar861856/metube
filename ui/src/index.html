How to download

  • 📋
    Copy the video, playlist, or channel URL from your source platform.
  • 📥
    Paste the URL into MeTube and choose your desired format or quality.
  • ⚙️
    Choose output options for captions, thumbnails, or specific codecs.
  • 🛠️
    Use Advanced settings to upload a cookies.txt file for private or logged-in content.
  • 🚀
    Queue the download and watch the progress in the downloader workspace.

Need to download private content? Install the Get cookies.txt LOCALLY browser extension, export your cookies as a .txt file, and upload it in the Advanced settings.
